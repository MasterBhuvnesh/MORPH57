Detailed:

MORPH57 is an AI-powered resume builder designed to eliminate the pain of tailoring resumes for every job application.

The problem: Job seekers spend hours manually tweaking resumes for each application, often missing critical ATS keywords, formatting inconsistently, and guessing what recruiters want to see.

The solution: Paste a job description, and MORPH57's AI instantly generates a professionally formatted, ATS-optimized resume tailored to that specific role. It analyzes the JD, identifies key skills and requirements, and structures your experience to match what hiring systems and recruiters are looking for.

Key features:
- Instant resume generation from any job description
- ATS-optimized formatting that passes automated screening
- Multiple professional templates to match different industries
- Smart JD parsing that highlights what matters
- Resume history so you can track and revisit past applications
- Export in multiple formats (PDF and source files)

Who it's for: Job seekers, career switchers, students entering the workforce, and anyone tired of spending more time formatting resumes than actually applying.

The vision: One paste, one click, one perfect resume — every time.
