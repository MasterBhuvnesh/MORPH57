# MORPH57

## One-liner

MORPH57 is an AI-powered resume builder that transforms job descriptions into ATS-optimized resumes instantly. Paste a JD, pick a template, and get a tailored, professional resume ready to submit — no formatting, no guesswork, just results.

## What is MORPH57?

MORPH57 is an AI-powered resume builder designed to eliminate the pain of tailoring resumes for every job application.

## The Problem

Job seekers spend hours manually tweaking resumes for each application, often missing critical ATS keywords, formatting inconsistently, and guessing what recruiters want to see.

## The Solution

Paste a job description, and MORPH57's AI instantly generates a professionally formatted, ATS-optimized resume tailored to that specific role. It analyzes the JD, identifies key skills and requirements, and structures your experience to match what hiring systems and recruiters are looking for.

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Instant Generation | Paste a JD and get a tailored resume in seconds |
| ATS-Optimized | Formatting and keywords designed to pass automated screening |
| Multiple Templates | Professional templates for different industries and roles |
| Smart JD Parsing | AI highlights and extracts what matters from job descriptions |
| Resume History | Track, revisit, and manage all your past resumes |
| Multiple Exports | Download as PDF or source files |

## Who is it for?

| Audience | Why MORPH57 helps |
|----------|-------------------|
| Job seekers | Stop spending hours formatting — focus on applying |
